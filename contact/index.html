Impressum

I 'm a pianist, composer and piano teacher,  thinking in melodies and breathing with notes.  My Genre is classical music but for events I can also play some Jazz music. I' m composing  film music and contemporary music. My compositions are written for Piano, Strings and Vocals.  When I teach piano my destination is to bring you into the marvelous music world. In my lessons it`s really important that everyone can enjoy learning an instrument. We will also create music to support your musical fantasy and connect it with the basics. 
"Composing is always a Journey into a mysterious sound world." - Melissa  ​
